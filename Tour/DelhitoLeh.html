Tour Description

Here’s a 10-day, 9-night itinerary for your Delhi to for ₹/-, covering the mesmerizing landscapes of Himachal Pradesh and Leh, including a mix of adventure, culture, and breathtaking scenery.

- ₹/-

Day 1: Delhi to Shimla (Arrival & Relaxation)

Morning:
Departure from Delhi to Shimla: Start your journey early in the morning (around 7:00 AM) via a comfortable Volvo bus or private vehicle. The journey will take around 7-8 hours. Breakfast and lunch stops will be along the way at designated rest areas.

Afternoon:
Arrival in Shimla: Arrive in Shimla by late afternoon and check into your hotel. Relax and refresh after your long journey.

Evening:
Explore Mall Road: In the evening, take a stroll on the famous Mall Road, where you can shop for souvenirs, woolen clothes, and handicrafts. Enjoy dinner at a local restaurant.

Night:
Overnight Stay in Shimla.

Day 2: Shimla to Kaza

Morning:
Breakfast & Check-out: After breakfast, check out from the hotel and start your journey to Kaza (about 200 km; 9-10 hours drive). The drive takes you through scenic mountains and quaint villages, crossing Narkanda, Rampur, and Reckong Peo.

Evening:
Arrival in Kaza: Arrive by evening and check into your hotel. Enjoy the tranquil mountain atmosphere and relax after the long journey.

Night:
Overnight Stay in Kaza.

Day 3: Kaza Local Sightseeing

Morning:
Visit Key Monastery: After breakfast, visit Key Monastery, perched on a hill, offering spectacular views of the Spiti Valley. Explore the monastery and soak in the peaceful atmosphere.

Afternoon:
Visit Kibber Village: Afterward, head to Kibber, one of the highest villages in the world. Explore the village and enjoy the panoramic views of the Spiti Valley.

Evening:
Relax in Kaza: Spend some time relaxing and enjoying the cool, serene environment of Kaza.

Night:
Overnight Stay in Kaza.

Day 4: Kaza to Kalpa

Morning:
Breakfast & Departure: After breakfast, check out and begin your journey to Kalpa (around 190 km; 8 hours drive). En route, enjoy the scenic drive through Nako Village, famous for its Buddhist monastery and tranquil surroundings.

Afternoon:
Arrival in Kalpa: Arrive in Kalpa by evening, a beautiful village nestled in the Kinnaur Valley, known for its stunning views of Kinner Kailash range.

Evening:
Relax and take in the views of the snow-capped peaks.

Night:
Overnight Stay in Kalpa.

Day 5: Kalpa to Leh (via Manali)

Morning:
Breakfast & Departure: After an early breakfast, embark on your journey to Leh (approximately 350 km; 12-14 hours drive). This is a long and challenging drive, crossing high mountain passes and offering breathtaking views of the Himalayas.

Afternoon/Evening:
Arrival in Leh: Reach Leh in the evening. Check into your hotel and relax after the long, exhausting journey.

Night:
Overnight Stay in Leh.

Day 6: Leh Local Sightseeing

Morning:
Visit Leh Palace: Start your day with a visit to the Leh Palace, an ancient Tibetan-style palace offering panoramic views of Leh town.

Afternoon:
Visit Shanti Stupa: Head to Shanti Stupa, a peaceful place with views of the Leh valley and a place to meditate.

Evening:
Explore Leh Market: Stroll around the local Leh Market to experience the vibrant culture, shop for souvenirs, and sample local food.

Night:
Overnight Stay in Leh.

Day 7: Leh to Nubra Valley

Morning:
Breakfast & Departure: After breakfast, depart for Nubra Valley (approximately 150 km; 5-6 hours drive). On the way, stop to visit the Khardung La Pass, one of the highest motorable passes in the world.

Afternoon:
Arrival in Nubra Valley: Reach Nubra Valley and check into your camp or hotel.

Evening:
Visit the famous Hunder Sand Dunes, where you can enjoy a camel ride on the sand dunes, surrounded by stunning mountains.

Night:
Overnight Stay in Nubra Valley.

Day 8: Nubra Valley to Pangong Lake

Morning:
Breakfast & Departure: After breakfast, head towards the mesmerizing Pangong Lake (about 200 km; 7-8 hours drive). Enjoy the scenic landscapes as you pass through Shayok Valley and reach the stunning blue waters of Pangong Lake.

Afternoon:
Explore Pangong Lake: Upon arrival, take a walk around the lake or enjoy a boat ride.

Evening:
Relax at Pangong Lake: Spend time by the lake, soaking in the peaceful beauty of the surroundings.

Night:
Overnight Stay at Pangong Lake.

Day 9: Pangong Lake to Leh

Morning:
Breakfast & Departure: After breakfast, begin your journey back to Leh via Chang La Pass.

Afternoon:
Arrival in Leh: Check-in to your hotel and relax after the day’s journey.

Evening:
Explore Leh, perhaps visit a local café for dinner or take a stroll around town.

Night:
Overnight Stay in Leh.

Day 10: Leh to Delhi (Return Journey)

Morning:
Breakfast & Check-out: After breakfast, check out from your hotel and proceed to the Leh Airport for your flight back to Delhi.

Afternoon/Evening:
Arrival in Delhi: Reach Delhi by late afternoon or evening, marking the end of your unforgettable journey.

Summary of the 10-Day Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Depart from Delhi and arrive in Shimla.
  • Day 2: Travel from Shimla to Kaza.
  • Day 3: Explore Kaza, Key Monastery, and Kibber Village.
  • Day 4: Drive from Kaza to Kalpa, exploring Nako Village on the way.
  • Day 5: Drive from Kalpa to Leh.
  • Day 6: Explore Leh, visit Leh Palace, Shanti Stupa, and Leh Market.
  • Day 7: Drive from Leh to Nubra Valley, visit Khardung La and Hunder Sand Dunes.
  • Day 8: Drive from Nubra Valley to Pangong Lake.
  • Day 9: Return from Pangong Lake to Leh.
  • Day 10: Flight from Leh to Delhi.

Tour Inclusions:
Accommodation: 9 nights in comfortable hotels/camps (on a twin-sharing basis).
Meals: Daily breakfast and dinner at the hotel/camps.
Transport: Comfortable vehicle for the entire trip (by road).
Sightseeing: All sightseeing as per the itinerary.
Driver/Guide: Professional driver for the trip.
Price: ₹44,999/- per person (on a twin-sharing basis)

Travel Tips:
Clothing: Pack for cold weather, as temperatures can drop significantly in higher altitudes. Warm layers, gloves, and jackets are essential.
Altitude Sickness: Acclimatize to the high altitudes, especially when traveling to Leh and Pangong Lake. Stay hydrated and avoid over-exertion.
Adventure Activities: Pre-book any adventure activities like camel rides or boat rides at Pangong Lake to avoid last-minute rush.
Health & Safety: Carry necessary medicines, a first-aid kit, and snacks for the journey.

This 10-day itinerary will let you experience the beauty of the Himalayas, the tranquility of Leh, and the rugged charm of Spiti Valley and Kaza, making for an unforgettable journey!
