Discover Chandigarh: The City Beautiful

Chandigarh, known as “The City Beautiful,” is famous for its modern architecture, wide green spaces, and unique design. As the capital of Punjab and Haryana, it is one of India’s most well-planned cities, offering a blend of urban lifestyle and natural beauty.

Rock Garden: Visit the famous Rock Garden of Chandigarh, an eclectic collection of sculptures made from scrap and industrial waste. It’s a must-see attraction in the city.

Sukhna Lake: Enjoy a peaceful boat ride at Sukhna Lake, a man-made reservoir located at the foothills of the Himalayas. It’s a popular spot for relaxation and leisure activities.

Rose Garden: The Zakir Hussain Rose Garden is one of the largest rose gardens in Asia, featuring a vast collection of roses and medicinal plants. It's a perfect place to enjoy nature and unwind.

Sector 17 Plaza: For those who love shopping, Sector 17 Plaza is the place to be. From branded stores to local markets, you can find everything you need here.

Punjab University Campus: Explore the beautifully designed campus of Punjab University, known for its architecture and green spaces. It's an ideal spot for a relaxing walk or photo op.

Chandigarh is not just a city of modern amenities but also a place that blends urban life with natural beauty. With so much to explore, it is truly a destination worth visiting!
